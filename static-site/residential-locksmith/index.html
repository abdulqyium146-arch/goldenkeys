Deadbolt Grades — What Dallas Homeowners Should Know

Not all deadbolts are created equal. The American National Standards Institute (ANSI) rates deadbolts on a scale of 1–3:

  • Grade 1: Highest security. Withstands 10 strikes and 250,000 open/close cycles. Recommended for all exterior doors.
  • Grade 2: Heavy-duty residential. Suitable for most homes. Common in Kwikset and Schlage mid-range lines.
  • Grade 3: Light duty. Interior doors or light-use applications only.

We recommend Schlage B60N or Kwikset 980 (both Grade 1) for Dallas front doors. For premium security, Medeco or Mul-T-Lock high-security deadbolts are available.

Smart Locks for Dallas Homes

Smart locks combine convenience with security. Key features to look for:

  • Multiple access codes for family, guests, housekeepers
  • Remote lock/unlock via smartphone
  • Activity log showing who entered and when
  • Auto-lock after a set time
  • Works with Alexa, Google Home, Apple HomeKit
  • Physical key backup in case of battery or Wi-Fi failure

Top brands we install: Schlage Encode, August Smart Lock Pro, Yale Assure Lock, Kwikset Halo. See our smart lock installation page for full details.
