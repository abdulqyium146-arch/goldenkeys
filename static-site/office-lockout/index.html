What to Expect During an Office Lockout Service

1

Call & Confirm Details

Call (214) 555-0147. Provide your exact address, floor/suite number, type of lock (if known), your name and phone. We dispatch the nearest available technician.

2

Authorization Verification

When our technician arrives, we verify your authorization to enter: business card + photo ID, property lease, or building manager authorization. This protects the business and ensures we're opening the right suite.

3

Non-Destructive Entry

We open the lock using picking, decoding, or bypass techniques appropriate for the lock type — without drilling or damaging the hardware when possible.

4

Recommended Next Steps

If the lockout was caused by lost keys, we recommend same-visit rekeying ($15–$35/lock). If the lock is worn or damaged, we recommend replacement. We perform both services on the spot.
