House Lockout Service in Dallas TX

Being locked out of your home is stressful, especially late at night or in bad weather. Golden's Casa Linda Keys provides fast, professional house lockout service across all of Dallas and surrounding cities. Our licensed technicians arrive quickly and use non-destructive entry techniques to get you back inside without damaging your door, frame, or lock.

🔒 Non-Destructive Entry First: We always attempt to open locks using picking, bypass, or shimming techniques before considering any destructive methods. In the vast majority of residential lockouts, no damage occurs to the lock or door.

Who We Help

🏠 Homeowners

Locked out of your house — any time of day or night. We verify ownership and get you in fast.

🏢 Renters

Apartment and condo tenants. Bring your lease or landlord authorization plus photo ID.

🔐 After Break-In

Door forced or lock damaged? We do emergency lock changes after a break-in or attempted entry.

What to Do While Waiting

  • Stay in a well-lit, safe area — near a neighbor or lit business if it's nighttime
  • Keep your phone charged and our number ready: (214) 555-0147
  • Have your photo ID ready — we verify ownership before entry
  • Do not attempt to force entry — you risk damaging the door, lock, and frame
  • Do not let strangers into your home while waiting for the locksmith
