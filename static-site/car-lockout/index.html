What NOT to Do When Locked Out of Your Car

❌ Don't Break the Window

Window replacement costs $150–$450 — far more than locksmith service. Tempered glass also shatters into hundreds of pieces creating a cleanup hazard. Only break a window if there's an immediate life-threatening emergency.

❌ Don't Use a Coat Hanger

Modern car doors have complex mechanisms behind weather stripping. Amateur slim jim attempts frequently scratch paint, bend weather seals (costing $50–$200 to replace), or damage internal door rods. Professional tools are designed to avoid these contact points.

❌ Don't Wait in an Unsafe Location

If you're in an isolated area, parking lot, or along a highway, move to a well-lit public location if possible. Wait inside a nearby store or restaurant. Inform someone of your location.

❌ Don't Trust Unmarked Vehicles

Legitimate locksmiths arrive in marked vehicles and present TACLA license before work begins. Ask to see credentials. We arrive in a clearly marked Golden's Casa Linda Keys van with licensed technicians.
